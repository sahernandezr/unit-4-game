Gem Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gems. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win by matching your total score to the random number and lose if your total score goes over.

If you win or lose, the game restarts with a new random number and new values for the four gems.
